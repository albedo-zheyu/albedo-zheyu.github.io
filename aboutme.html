About Me

I am currently a Master of Engineering (Telecommunications) candidate at the University of New South Wales (UNSW), Sydney, expected to graduate in March 2026.

My current master's thesis focuses on automated protocol modelling for IoT systems. I design pipelines that employ lightweight local large language models (LLMs), enhanced with retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), to generate machine-readable protocol specifications in JSON format directly from RFC documents. These models can be applied to raw network traffic for deep flow inspection (DFI), supporting packet parsing and protocol identification with implications for network monitoring and cybersecurity.

I am seeking PhD opportunities in data science and related areas. My research interests include machine learning for communication networks, automated protocol analysis, and data-driven methods for networked systems.
